Gangtok

After we went to Kolkata, we went to Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim. Sikkim is a mountainous state located between Nepal, China, and Bhutan, known for its organic farming and natural beauty. While we were staying in Gangtok, we travelled went to Pakyong airport and listened to a talk about the airport’s design. We also went to the Rumtek monastery, Tsmogo glacial lake, and explored downtown Gangtok.
