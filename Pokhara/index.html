Nepal | 5/27/19 - 5/29/19

Pokhara

Pokhara is a city in the Gandaki Pradesh province in Nepal, located on the shore of Phewa Lake. We spent 2 days there, during which we visited a Tibetan refugee camp. Some of us woke up early to watch the sun rise over the Himalayas, and we all enjoyed the view of the mountains from the city.
